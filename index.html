About Andolinis

The Italian restaurant is located in Andover, MA. If you need a nice and calm place to eat with your family, or a few drinks to have after a long day at work, or even a banquet for an anniversary, Andolinis Restaurant is the perfect place in town to go to! They serve pasta, pizza, steak, salads and seafood. They even have a gluten free menu because they believe everyone has the right to enjoy their meals. The restaurant is open every day, even on holidays! Andolinis are continously looking to diverse their menu so that it can match everyone's desires, which is why they opened up the popular restaurant next to Andolinis, Sauce. As a promis, Andolinis will never disappoint your taste buds!

We are proud of what we do here!
